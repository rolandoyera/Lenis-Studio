# Emails

The branded transactional emails sent during the contract delivery + signing
flow, rendered from the production templates with mock data. They're built to
survive real email clients: table-based layouts with **inline styles only**
(Gmail/Outlook strip `<style>` blocks and external CSS), a white content card on
a tinted page, and a muted footer. The logo only renders when an absolute
`https` URL is configured; otherwise the header falls back to the company name.

These are the canonical email surfaces — **don't restyle them without
approval**, and keep both templates visually in sync.
